### Hi there 👋, I'm Bernardo Borges Sandoval

I'm a software developer with experience in Physical Design (mainly OpenROAD), as well as a researcher with a background in soft errors.

#### Academic Background
- I'm currently a Master's student at [UFSC](https://en.ufsc.br/).
- I have a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from [UFSC](https://en.ufsc.br/).
- I'm an Electronics Technician, having graduated from [IFSC](https://www.ifsc.edu.br/).

#### Work Experience
- I worked part-time as a software developer on the [OpenROAD Project](https://github.com/The-OpenROAD-Project) for one year, with an emphasis on the Pin Access stage of Detailed Routing.
- I completed an internship at [IMEC](https://www.imec-int.com/en), where I enabled the N2 PDK in the OpenROAD flow.
- I'm currently back at the OpenROAD Project, applying my newly acquired experience to improve Detailed Routing and enable the OR tool on advanced technology nodes.
